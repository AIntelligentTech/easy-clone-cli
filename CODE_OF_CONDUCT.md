# Contributor Covenant Code of Conduct

## Our Commitment

We are committed to providing a welcoming and inspiring community for all. We pledge that everyone participating in the Easy Clone CLI community and its communities will be treated with respect and dignity.

## Our Standards

Examples of behavior that contributes to creating a positive environment include:

- Using welcoming and inclusive language
- Being respectful of differing opinions, viewpoints, and experiences
- Gracefully accepting constructive criticism
- Focusing on what is best for the community
- Showing empathy towards other community members
- Assuming good faith in others' intentions

Examples of unacceptable behavior by participants include:

- Deliberate intimidation, threats, or violent language
- Discriminatory jokes and language
- Posting or displaying sexually explicit or violent material
- Posting or threatening to post others' personally identifying information
- Personal attacks or insults, especially those using racist or sexist slurs
- Unwelcome sexual attention or advances
- Harassment of any kind, public or private
- Publishing others' private information, such as physical or electronic address, without explicit permission
- Other conduct which could reasonably be considered inappropriate in a professional setting
- Advocating for or encouraging any of the above behaviors

## Enforcement Responsibilities

Community leaders are responsible for:
- Clarifying and enforcing our standards of acceptable behavior
- Taking appropriate and fair corrective action in response to behavior violations
- Removing, editing, or rejecting comments, commits, code, wiki edits, issues, and other contributions
- Banning temporarily or permanently any contributor whose behavior they deem inappropriate

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies to:
- All project spaces (GitHub issues, discussions, pull requests, wiki)
- Official community events and forums
- Any action or speech representing the project while in a position of authority

## Enforcement

Instances of abusive, harassing, or otherwise unacceptable behavior may be reported by:
- Contacting the project maintainers
- Using the GitHub report function
- Emailing with details of the violation

All complaints will be reviewed and investigated promptly and fairly.

## Consequences

Depending on the severity, consequences may include:
- A private verbal warning
- A public warning
- A request to cease violating behavior
- A temporary or permanent ban from the community
